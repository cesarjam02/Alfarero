About ALFARERO

A colorful neighborhood cake shop where coffee, stories, and fresh bakes come together.

A little pastel corner in the city

ALFARERO CAKE SHOP was imagined as a calm, pastel-colored pause button in the middle of busy days. We mix the warmth of a family bakery with the energy of a modern café: soft playlists, colorful mugs, and the smell of something just-out-of-the-oven.

Every detail, from the plates to the plants, is chosen so that you and your friends feel like staying “just 10 minutes more” — for one more coffee, one more slice, or one more conversation.

Handcrafted cakes

Colorful celebration cakes, tiny cupcakes, and seasonal specials made in small batches.

Friendly corners

Cozy corners for reading, big tables for study groups, and window seats for people-watching.

Our sweet philosophy

Simple recipes, bright colors, and a lot of care — everything designed to make your day a little softer.

Small-batch baking

We prefer baking smaller, more often, so that the display case always feels alive and changing.

Color that feels calm

Pastel tones on the walls, bright plates, and tiny details that make every photo feel warm.

Community-first

From quiet laptop mornings to birthday afternoons, we want ALFARERO to feel like “your” spot.
